Momma's Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread has been a longstanding tradition and treat in the Healy household. Both on wonderfully warm fall days, but also fridgid cold winters, banana bread always seemed to make an appearance. Over the years my mom has perfected the recipe and now I'll be sharing them here, for the world to see.

Just to make a side note about it, this is a recipe that you can have a lot of fun with if you're really into bananas, then go ahead and add some extra! Think it needs to be sweeter, maybe some more sugar is your route. This recipe isn't an exact science, so just have fun with it!

    Ingredients

  • 4-5 Mushy Bananas
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Milk

    Steps

  1. Take the bananas and mush them into a pile
  2. from there you can go and add them to a mixing bowl with your wet ingredients
  3. On the side, mix all of the dry ingredients together
  4. Slowly incorporiate the dry ingredients into the wet mixture
  5. Grease a breadpan, then preheat the oven to 400 degrees and cook for 20 minutes
