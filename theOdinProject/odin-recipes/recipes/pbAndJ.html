Lunchtime PB and J

Description

A lunch room classic is the PB+J, one that has taken many forms and has been at the center of many of our childhoods. Something like this doesn't take a lot of specific measurements, and all of the ingredients are in the name! But if you need the help, follow along and we will get through it together!

    Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread out
  2. Add a thing layer of peanut butter to both pieces
  3. Then spread jelly across one of the sides
  4. Close the pieces then cut and serve!
