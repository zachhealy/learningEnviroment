Momma's Banana Bread

Description

Coffee is the daily starter for most of America, this is one of my favorite recipes that has got me feeling amped in the mornig to get going. While it is a simple recipe, it is one that is also experimental depending on you and your personal taste buds!

    Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Creamer (to taste)
  • French Vanilla Syrup
  • Optional: Ice

    Steps

  1. Brew your coffee into a desired cup
  2. From there, add cream and sugar to your liking
  3. Then take your syrup and add however much you like
  4. If you want it iced, simply pour it over ice once you're done!
